By using Unity's Mecanim Animator system and animation events, creating a modular battle system with unique attacks is greatly simplified.

Attacks are randomly chosen, and can use projectiles, either using GameObjects or particles.

Enemy attacks can either be direct or through projectiles. The player can dodge them, or they can catch the projectiles to fill a meter.

By filling the meter, the player's attack the next turn is strengthened.
